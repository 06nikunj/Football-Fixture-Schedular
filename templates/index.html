How Backtracking Works
Each match is placed one by one into a day/slot. Before placing, 3 constraints are checked: no consecutive days, one match per slot per day, and balanced home/away. If any fails → the algorithm backtracks and tries the next slot.
📅

No Consecutive Days

A team cannot play on back-to-back days — rest is enforced.

🕐

One Slot Per Day

Each team plays at most once per day. Each slot holds one match.

⚖️

Home / Away Balance

Every team plays each opponent once at home and once away.
